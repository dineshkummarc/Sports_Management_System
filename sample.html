Moreover, based on the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO), here are some reminders in playing the Swertres hearing:

  1. Only players 18 years old and above are allowed to play the lotto games.
  2. This lotto game has daily draws every 2:00 pm, 5:00 pm, and 9:00 pm. You can watch the live draw through YouTube.
  3. In case you got the three winning numbers in exact order, you will win Php 4,500.00.
  4. If you are playing via the Rambolito, you may win:
    • Rambolito 3 – Php 1,500.00
    • Rambolito 6 – Php 750.00

How To Play the PCSO Swertres Hearing

  1. Pick three(3) digits that will compose your combination. You will have to choose from numbers 0-9, one(1) number for each column.
  2. If you don’t have a combination in mind, you may also choose the Lucky Pick which is a randomly generated combination of digits.
  3. For the Rambolito system play, you may choose three(3) digits and the system will automatically generate the possible combinations with regards to the digits you have chosen.
